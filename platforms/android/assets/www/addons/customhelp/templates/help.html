FAQs
How can I reset/change my password?

Currently, there isn't an option to reset or change your password within this app. Please log in to mProve at https://Moodle.Markerstudy.com to change your password. If you have forgotten your password, please contact eTalent at eTalent@Markerstudy.com.

I can't access a course

If you can't access a course, it may be because the course is not available on Mobile devices. Alternatively, you may not be enrolled on the course. The course should give you some information regarding why access has been denied, but if you are unsure, please contact eTalent.

Course is not loading/Course crashes

Courses will automatically download to your device before they can begin. If the course has not downloaded, or has downloaded and is not loading, try synchronising with the eCloud within Settings. Alternatively, log out of the app and log back in again.

On completing a course, your completion may not immediately update. Please give the app up to 24 hours to register this. If your completion has still not registered after this time, please contact eTalent@Markerstudy.com.

Please note: There is a known issue with some courses crashing the app when completing. Your attempts and scores are registered, and any submitted data is not lost. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact eTalent@Markerstudy.com.

I can't find a course I'm looking for

Currently, not all courses are available through Mobile. To search courses, click the search button in the top right of the screen within 'My Courses'. If you believe the course should be available, please contact eTalent@Markerstudy.com.
